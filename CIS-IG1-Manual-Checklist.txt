============================================================
  CIS IG1 MANUAL ASSESSMENT CHECKLIST
  For items that cannot be evaluated by Collect.ps1
============================================================

PURPOSE
  Collect.ps1 covers all technical/measurable CIS IG1 safeguards at the
  endpoint level. The items in this checklist require interviews, document
  review, or access to systems outside the endpoint (identity provider,
  firewall, backup platform). Work through this checklist on-site with the
  owner or IT contact.

SCORING
  For each item mark one of:
    PASS     - Control is in place and functioning
    FAIL     - Control is absent or clearly not functioning
    PARTIAL  - Partially in place; gaps noted
    N/A      - Not applicable to this environment
    UNKNOWN  - Could not determine during this visit
